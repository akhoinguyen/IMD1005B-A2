Wanna drive on Mars?

I found this super cool website — slowroads.io — that allows you to drive enlessly through various terrains, with Mars being one of them. This website blew me away when I first discovered it, and I thought you might like it too. Not to mention, it's made using JavaScript and not some fancy outlandish technology. Makes me admire and jealous at the same time. Enjoy!

Slow Roads is a casual driving game all about finding peace in a long, scenic journey. No ads, no interruptions, and no end to the road. Set the weather to suit your mood, throw on some music, and just drive.
— Anslo, creator
