Things to Do on Mars.

Grand Canyon of Mars Tour

Explore Valles Marineris, a canyon so massive it spans almost the width of Canada from coast to coast! This extraordinary terrain is a must-see for any visitor up for a thrilling exploration.

Olympus Mons Hike

Embark on a journey to the tallest volcano in the Solar System! Sitting at an incredible 22-kilometre high, Olympus Mons towers almost three times our Mount Everest. With Mars' lower gravity, this hike, despite long, is not as strenuous as it sounds. At the summit, you'll be rewarded with a breathtaking view of the Martian landscape.

Rover Racing

Test your racing skills on the dusty racetracks! Rent a rover and compete against skilled racers in this thrilling sport. Navigate through rugged red terrain while dodging ancient dunes and crater rims. A tournament is rumoured to be held soon, so start practising now!
